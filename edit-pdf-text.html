Edit PDF Text

Add or replace text in your PDF. Click anywhere on the page to add a new text block. Note: This tool adds new text on top of the PDF; it does not edit the original text directly.

How to Edit PDF Text

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Click on a page to add a text box.
  3. Enter your text and press Enter.
  4. Click 'Save PDF' to download the updated file.

Edit PDF Text FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.
