Merge PDF Files

Easily combine multiple PDF documents into one single file. All processing happens securely in your browser, so your files remain private.

How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Select or drag and drop all the PDF files you want to combine.
  2. Reorder the files if necessary by dragging them.
  3. Click 'Process Files' to combine them.
  4. Your merged PDF will download automatically.

Merge PDF FAQs

Is there a limit on how many files I can merge?
There's no strict limit, but performance depends on your browser and computer for very large numbers of files.

Are my files uploaded to a server?
No. This tool processes your files directly in your web browser, ensuring maximum privacy.
