Add Page Numbers to PDF

Easily insert page numbers into your PDF document. Choose the position, and our tool will automatically add numbers to every page for you.

How to Add Page Numbers

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Choose the desired position for the page numbers (e.g., Bottom Center).
  3. Click 'Process Files'.
  4. Download your PDF with page numbers added.

Page Numbering FAQs

Can I customize the font or size?
Currently, we offer a standard format for simplicity, but we plan to add more customization options in the future.

Is the process secure?
Yes, page numbers are added directly in your browser, ensuring your document's privacy.
