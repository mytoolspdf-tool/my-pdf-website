How to Unlock a PDF

  1. Upload your password-protected PDF.

Unlock PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Do you store my password?
No. Your password is used only in your browser to decrypt the file and is never stored or transmitted.

What if I don't know the password?
You must know the current password to remove it. This tool cannot recover lost passwords.
