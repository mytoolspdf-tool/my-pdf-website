Resize PDF Page

Change the page size of your PDF document. Choose from standard sizes like A4 and Letter, or specify your own custom dimensions.

How to Resize a PDF Page

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select a new page size from the dropdown.
  3. If you choose 'Custom', enter the width and height in millimeters.
  4. Click 'Resize and Save PDF' to download the updated file.
