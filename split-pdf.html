Split PDF Files Securely

Split your PDF into single pages, by fixed ranges, or extract specific pages. Our free tool works in your browser, ensuring your files remain private.

How to Split a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Choose your preferred split mode (Extract, Single Pages, or Fixed Range).
  3. Enter the required page numbers or range size.
  4. Click 'Process Files' to start.
  5. Download your new PDF(s).

Split PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Will I get multiple files?
If you choose to split into single pages or by a fixed range, you will receive a ZIP file containing all the new PDFs. If you extract pages, you will receive a single new PDF.
