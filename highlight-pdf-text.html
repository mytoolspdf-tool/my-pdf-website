Highlight PDF Text

Easily highlight text in your PDF. Just draw a rectangle over the text you want to highlight, and it will be marked with a semi-transparent yellow color.

How to Highlight a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Draw a rectangle over the text you want to highlight.
  3. Click 'Save PDF' to download the updated file.
