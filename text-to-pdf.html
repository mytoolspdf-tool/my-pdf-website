Convert Text to PDF

Easily convert your plain text into a professional PDF document. All processing is done in your browser, so your files are never uploaded to a server.

How to Convert Text to PDF

  1. Paste your text into the text area.
  2. Click 'Convert to PDF' to start.
  3. Download your new PDF file.

Text to PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Can I use formatted text?
This tool currently supports plain text only. Any formatting will be removed during the conversion process.
