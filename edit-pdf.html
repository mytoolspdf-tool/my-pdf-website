Add Text to PDF

Need to fill out a form or add an annotation? This tool lets you add new text to any page of your PDF file, directly in your browser.

How to Add Text to a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF document.
  2. Enter the text you want to add, the page number, and the font size.
  3. Click 'Process Files'.
  4. A new PDF with your added text will be created for download.

Add Text FAQs

Can I move the text around?
Currently, you must specify the location programmatically. A visual editor is planned for a future update.

Is this secure?
Yes, all edits happen in your browser, ensuring the confidentiality of your document.
