Convert GIF to PDF

Easily convert your GIF images (including the first frame of animated GIFs) into a single PDF document. All processing is done in your browser, so your files are never uploaded to a server.

How to Convert GIF to PDF

  1. Upload one or more GIF files.
  2. Click 'Convert to PDF' to start.
  3. Download your new PDF file.

GIF to PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Does it work with animated GIFs?
This tool will convert the first frame of an animated GIF into a static image in the PDF.
