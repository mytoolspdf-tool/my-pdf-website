    Troubleshooting Common Issues

    Annotation Placement Problems

    If annotations don't appear in the correct location, ensure you're clicking or drawing precisely where you want the markup to appear. The tool places annotations based on your exact cursor position.

    Color Visibility Issues

    If annotations are difficult to see, try using contrasting colors that stand out against the document background. Red, blue, and green typically provide good visibility on most documents.

    Text Annotation Problems

    For text annotations, ensure you click precisely where you want to place the text note. The annotation will appear at your click location and can be positioned for optimal readability.
