Annotate PDF

Add drawings and text annotations to your PDF documents. Mark up, comment, and highlight with ease.

How to Annotate a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select a tool (draw or text) and a color.
  3. Click or draw on the page to add your annotation.
  4. Click 'Save PDF' to download the updated file.
