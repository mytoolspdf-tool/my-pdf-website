Troubleshooting Common Issues

Incorrect Page Positioning

If blank pages appear in wrong locations, verify your position input. Remember that position 0 inserts at the beginning, and other numbers insert after the specified page.

Page Count Discrepancies

If the wrong number of blank pages are inserted, check your quantity input and ensure you're specifying the correct number of pages to add.

Format Inconsistencies

Blank pages automatically match the original document's page size and orientation. If formatting appears inconsistent, verify your original document has uniform page formatting.
