Best Practices for Effective Redaction

🎯 Professional Redaction Guidelines

Complete Coverage: Ensure redaction blocks completely cover all sensitive text with no partial characters visible.

Consistent Application: Apply the same redaction standards throughout all documents in a case or project.

Verification Process: Always review redacted documents to confirm no sensitive information remains visible or recoverable.
