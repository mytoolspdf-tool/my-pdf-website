Redact PDF Securely

Permanently black out sensitive information in your PDF documents. This tool ensures that confidential data like names, addresses, and social security numbers are completely removed and unrecoverable.

How to Redact a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Draw rectangles over the text or images you want to redact.
  3. Click 'Redact PDF' to apply the changes permanently.
  4. Download your secure, redacted PDF.

PDF Redaction FAQs

Is the redacted content truly unrecoverable?
Yes. Unlike simply placing a black box over text, our tool removes the underlying content from the PDF, making it impossible to recover.

Is this process secure?
Absolutely. Your files are processed entirely in your browser, meaning they are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your sensitive data remains private.
