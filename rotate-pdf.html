Troubleshooting Common Issues

Incorrect Rotation Results

If pages rotate in unexpected directions, verify your angle selection. Remember that 270¬∞ clockwise equals 90¬∞ counter-clockwise for achieving the desired orientation.

Page Selection Problems

For page selection issues, use proper syntax: individual pages ("1, 3, 5"), ranges ("2-8"), or combinations ("1, 3-5, 9"). Spaces around commas and hyphens are optional.

Large File Processing

Very large PDF files may take longer to process. Ensure your browser has sufficient memory available and consider splitting large documents for faster processing.
