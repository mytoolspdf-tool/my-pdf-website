Rotate PDF Pages Securely

Rotate your PDF files for free with our secure, in-browser tool. Choose to rotate all pages or select specific pages to adjust their orientation instantly.

How to Rotate a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Choose your preferred rotation angle.
  3. Enter the page numbers you want to rotate (e.g., "1, 3-5, 8"). Leave blank to rotate all pages.
  4. Click 'Process Files' to start.
  5. Download your newly rotated PDF.

Rotate PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Can I rotate only specific pages?
Yes, you can specify which pages to rotate by entering the page numbers in the input field. If you leave it blank, all pages will be rotated.
