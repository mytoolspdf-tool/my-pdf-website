Word to PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, we use secure HTTPS connections. Your files are encrypted during transfer and automatically deleted from our servers after one hour.

Will my formatting be preserved?
Our tool strives to maintain your original formatting, but complex layouts may have minor differences. The PDF will be a static, non-editable version of your document.
