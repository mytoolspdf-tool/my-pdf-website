Crop PDF Pages

Visually crop your PDF pages. Upload a PDF, draw a box on each page to select the area you want to keep, and download the cropped file.

How to Crop a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Draw a rectangle on each page to define the crop area.
  3. Click 'Crop and Download PDF' to get your new file.
