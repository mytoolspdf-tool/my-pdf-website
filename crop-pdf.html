If crop areas don't appear correctly, ensure you're drawing rectangles precisely over the content you want to keep. The tool crops based on your drawn boundaries.

If important content gets cut off, review your crop area boundaries and ensure they encompass all essential text and images with appropriate margins.

Quality Degradation

Cropping maintains original quality, but very small crop areas may result in content that's difficult to read. Ensure crop areas are appropriately sized for intended use.
