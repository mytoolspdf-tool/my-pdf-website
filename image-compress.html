Compress JPG or PNG

Reduce the file size of your JPG or PNG images with our free online optimizer. Perfect for making your images load faster on the web.

How to Compress an Image

  1. Upload your JPG or PNG image.
  2. Choose a compression level (Low, Medium, High).
  3. Click 'Process Files' to start the optimization.
  4. Download your new, smaller image file.

Image Compression FAQs

What's the difference between compression levels?
High compression gives you the smallest file size but may reduce quality. Low compression provides better quality with a larger file size.

Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Your files are uploaded via a secure connection and are deleted from our servers immediately after processing.
