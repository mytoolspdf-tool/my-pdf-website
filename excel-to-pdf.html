Convert your Excel spreadsheets to high-quality PDF files for free. Our online tool makes it easy to share and archive your data securely.

How to Convert Excel to PDF

  1. Upload your Excel file.
  2. Choose your page orientation (Portrait or Landscape).
  3. Click 'Process Files' to start the conversion.
  4. Download your newly created PDF.

Excel to PDF FAQs

Why does my formatting look different?
The converter does its best to preserve formatting, but complex spreadsheets may have minor differences. We recommend reviewing the PDF after conversion.

Are my files secure?
Yes, your files are uploaded over a secure connection and are deleted from our servers immediately after processing.
