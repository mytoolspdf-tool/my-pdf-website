Auto-Detect and Remove Blank Pages from PDF

This tool scans your PDF for blank pages and removes them automatically, creating a cleaner, more compact document.

How to Remove Blank Pages

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Click 'Detect and Remove Blank Pages'.
  3. The tool will process the file and automatically download a new PDF with the blank pages removed.

Remove Blank Pages FAQs

How does it detect blank pages?
The tool renders each page and analyzes it to see if it's empty. A small tolerance is used to account for minor scanning artifacts.

Is my data safe?
Yes, all processing happens in your browser. Your files are not uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.
