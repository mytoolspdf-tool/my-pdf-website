Troubleshooting Common Issues

Link Placement Problems

If hyperlinks don't appear in the correct location, ensure you're drawing the link area precisely over the intended text or image. The tool creates clickable areas based on your drawn rectangles.

URL Functionality Issues

If links don't work properly, verify that URLs are complete and correctly formatted. Include the full protocol (http:// or https://) for web links and proper formatting for email links (mailto:).

Reader Compatibility

Some older PDF readers may have limited hyperlink support. Test your interactive PDFs in multiple readers to ensure broad compatibility and functionality.
