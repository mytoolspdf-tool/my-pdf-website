Add Hyperlinks to PDF

Easily add clickable hyperlinks to your PDF. Draw a box over the area you want to link, enter a URL, and save your updated document.

How to Add Hyperlinks to a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Draw a rectangle on the page to define the link area.
  3. Enter the URL when prompted.
  4. Click 'Save PDF' to download the updated file.
