Add Header and Footer to PDF

Easily add a custom header and footer to every page of your PDF document. All processing is done securely in your browser.

How to Add a Header and Footer

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Enter the desired text for the header and/or footer.
  3. Click 'Add Header/Footer' to process the file.
  4. Download your updated PDF.

Header and Footer FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.
