Troubleshooting Common Issues

Text Overlap Problems

If headers or footers overlap with document content, consider shortening the text or adjusting the content to ensure proper spacing and readability.

Formatting Inconsistencies

Headers and footers are applied uniformly to all pages. If formatting appears inconsistent, verify your original document has uniform page formatting and margins.

Character Display Issues

For special characters or non-English text, ensure your browser supports Unicode rendering. Most modern browsers handle international characters correctly in headers and footers.
