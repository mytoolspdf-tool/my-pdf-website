Create PDF Booklet

This tool rearranges the pages of your PDF to create a booklet, ready for printing and binding.

How to Create a PDF Booklet

  1. Upload your PDF file. The number of pages will be padded to a multiple of 4 if needed.
  2. Click 'Create and Save Booklet' to download the reordered PDF.
  3. When printing, choose two-sided printing, flipping on the short edge.
