Flatten PDF

Make your PDF's annotations and form fields permanent and uneditable by flattening the document. All processing is done securely in your browser.

How to Flatten a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Click 'Flatten PDF' to start the process.
  3. Download your new, flattened PDF file.

Flatten PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

What does 'flattening' do?
Flattening a PDF merges interactive elements like annotations and form fields into the main content of the page, making them non-interactive.
