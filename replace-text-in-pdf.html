Replace Text in PDF

Find and replace all occurrences of specific text in your PDF file. A powerful tool for making bulk updates to your documents, such as changing names, dates, or correcting recurring errors.

How to Replace Text in a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Enter the text you want to find in the 'Find' field.
  3. Enter the replacement text in the 'Replace with' field.
  4. Click 'Replace Text' to process the file.
  5. Download your updated PDF.

Replace Text FAQs

Does this tool work with scanned PDFs?
This tool works best with text-based PDFs. It cannot replace text in images or scanned documents.

Is this process secure?
Yes. Your files are processed directly in your browser and are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your data remains private.
