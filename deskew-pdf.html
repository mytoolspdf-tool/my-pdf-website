Deskew PDF Online

Automatically straighten scanned pages in your PDF. Our tool corrects skewed images and text, improving readability and giving your documents a more professional appearance.

How to Deskew a PDF

  1. Upload your scanned PDF file.
  2. Click 'Straighten PDF' to begin the deskewing process.
  3. Our tool will automatically detect and correct the skew.
  4. Download your straightened, professional-looking PDF.

PDF Deskewing FAQs

What kind of PDFs work best?
This tool is designed for scanned documents that were not perfectly aligned during scanning. It works best on documents with clear text lines.

Is this process secure?
Yes. Your files are processed entirely in your browser and are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your data remains private.
