Troubleshooting Common Issues

Shape Placement Problems

If shapes don't appear in the correct location, ensure you're drawing precisely where you want the shape to appear. The tool creates shapes based on your drawn area boundaries.

Color Display Issues

If shape colors appear different than expected, verify your monitor color settings and test the document on different devices to ensure consistent color representation.

Size and Proportion Concerns

For optimal shape appearance, draw shapes proportionally to your content. Very small shapes may not be visible, while oversized shapes can overwhelm document content.
