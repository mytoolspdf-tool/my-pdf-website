Add Shapes to PDF

Draw shapes like rectangles and circles directly onto your PDF pages. Customize the color and position of your shapes with ease.

How to Add Shapes to a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select a shape type and color.
  3. Draw on the page where you want to add the shape.
  4. Click 'Save PDF' to download the updated file.
