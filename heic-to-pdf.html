HEIC/HEIF to PDF FAQs

Why should I convert HEIC to PDF?
HEIC is a great format, but not all devices can open it. Converting to PDF makes your images viewable on any platform.

Is this process secure?
Yes. All image processing happens directly in your browser, so your files are never uploaded to a server.
