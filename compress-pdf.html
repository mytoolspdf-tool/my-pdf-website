Compress PDF

Reduce the file size of your PDF documents while optimizing for the best possible quality. This makes your files easier to email and share.

How to Compress a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Choose a compression level (Low, Medium, or High).
  3. Click 'Process Files' to send the file to our secure server for compression.
  4. Download your new, smaller PDF file.

Compress PDF FAQs

What's the difference between compression levels?
High compression results in the smallest file size but may reduce quality. Low compression provides better quality with a larger file size.

Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Your files are uploaded via a secure connection and are deleted from our servers immediately after processing.
