💡 Pro Tips for Best Results

Get the most out of your JPG to PDF conversions with these expert recommendations:

Optimize Image Quality: Use high-resolution JPGs (300 DPI) for professional printing, but compress for web sharing to reduce file size.
Smart Organization: Name your images logically before uploading (01-cover, 02-intro, etc.) for easier arrangement in the final PDF.
Choose Right Orientation: Use portrait for documents and photos, landscape for wide images like charts or panoramic shots.
Margin Magic: Add margins for professional binding, or use "none" for full-bleed images that fill the entire page.
Batch Processing: Convert multiple related images at once to maintain consistent formatting and save time.
