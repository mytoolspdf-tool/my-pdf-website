Edit PDF Metadata

Quickly edit the metadata of your PDF files. Change the title, author, subject, and keywords directly in your browser. Your files remain private and secure.

How to Edit PDF Metadata

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Modify the metadata fields as needed.
  3. Click 'Save Changes' to download the updated PDF.

Edit PDF Metadata FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

What metadata can I edit?
You can edit the Title, Author, Subject, and Keywords of the PDF document.
