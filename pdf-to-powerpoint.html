Convert PDF to PowerPoint

Convert your PDF files into editable PowerPoint presentations with our free online tool. Choose to keep the original formatting or reflow the text for easy editing.

How to Convert PDF to PowerPoint

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select your preferred layout option.
  3. Click 'Process Files' to start the conversion.
  4. Download your new PowerPoint presentation.

PDF to PowerPoint FAQs

Will my formatting be preserved?
You can choose to keep the source formatting, which preserves the original layout, or reflow the text for easier editing. Complex PDFs may require some adjustments.

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are uploaded over a secure connection and are deleted from our servers immediately after processing.
