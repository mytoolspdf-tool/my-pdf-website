Add Watermark to PDF

Protect your documents by adding a text watermark. Enter your desired text, and it will be applied to every page of your PDF file securely in your browser.

How to Add a Watermark

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. In the options, type the text you want to use as a watermark.
  3. Click 'Process Files' to apply it.
  4. Your watermarked PDF will be ready for download.

Watermark FAQs

Can I change the watermark's position or color?
The tool currently applies a standard, diagonally centered watermark for maximum visibility. More options are coming soon!

Are my files safe?
Yes, your files are processed in-browser and are not sent to any servers, keeping your information secure.
