N-Up PDF

This tool arranges multiple pages of your PDF onto a single page, perfect for creating handouts or saving paper.

How to Create an N-Up PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select how many pages you want per sheet (2, 4, 8, or 16).
  3. Click 'Create N-Up PDF' to download the combined file.

N-Up PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.
