N-Up PDF FAQs

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

What's the difference between each N-Up option?
2-up places 2 pages side by side, 4-up creates a 2x2 grid, 8-up arranges pages in a 4x2 layout, and 16-up creates a 4x4 grid on each sheet.

Will the quality be maintained?
Absolutely! Our tool maintains the original PDF quality while intelligently scaling pages to fit the selected layout.
