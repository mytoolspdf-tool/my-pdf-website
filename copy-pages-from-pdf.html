Copy Pages from PDF

This tool allows you to copy specific pages from a PDF, even multiple times, to create a new PDF document.

How to Copy Pages

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Enter the page numbers you want to copy. You can use commas for individual pages and hyphens for ranges (e.g., "1, 3, 5-7"). You can also list a page multiple times to duplicate it (e.g., "1, 1, 2").
  3. Click 'Copy Pages and Save' to download the new PDF.
