Convert PowerPoint to PDF

Convert your PowerPoint presentations into high-quality PDFs with our free online tool. Choose your page orientation and share your slides in a universal format.

How to Convert PowerPoint to PDF

  1. Upload your PowerPoint file.
  2. Choose your preferred page orientation.
  3. Click 'Process Files' to start the conversion.
  4. Download your new PDF document.

PowerPoint to PDF FAQs

Will my animations and transitions be included?
No, PDF is a static format and does not support animations or transitions. The PDF will contain the final state of each slide.

Is my data safe?
Yes, your files are uploaded over a secure connection and are deleted from our servers immediately after processing.
